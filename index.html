A Few Words About The House

Vila Fjorald

Situated in Çuke in the Vlore County region, with Mango Beach nearby, Villa Fjorald features accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking. Guests can relax in the garden at the property. Flamingo Beach is 2.2 km from the apartment, while Santa Quaranta Beach is 2.8 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 97 km from Villa Fjorald.

A truly exceptional villa not only boasts luxurious amenities and breathtaking views but also fosters a sense of serenity and exclusivity, ensuring that every guest feels truly pampered and rejuvenated during their stay. Whether it's lounging by the poolside oasis or exploring nearby attractions, each moment is imbued with a sense of luxury and unparalleled hospitality.
